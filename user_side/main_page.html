Our Story

Brewing the Dark Side Since 2010

Darth Coffee was founded in the heart of Gujarat with a simple mission: to bring the darkest, richest coffee experience to those who appreciate the bold side of life. What started as a small cafe with just three tables has grown into Ahmedabad's premier coffee destination.

Our founder, Darth Vader (yes, really!), was inspired by his travels across the galaxy to create a coffee experience unlike any other. Using beans sourced from the volcanic soils of Mustafar and perfected with our proprietary dark roasting process, we've created blends that awaken your senses.
